Top 5 attraction

  1. Mahakaleshwar :In Sanskrit language, Kala means both time and death. The one who defeats time and death both is known as Mahakal.
  2. Ram Ghat: A massive statue of Lord Hanuman stands in one corner of thee ghat, blessing everyone who visits it.
  3. Gopal Mandir: It is located in the main market of Ujjain. The temple is in Ujjain town which is 65 km away from Indore city; the city is situated in Madhya Pradesh.
  4. Harshidhi Mata Mandir: The presence of Shree Yantra which represents nine forms of Durga is one of the characteristic features of this temple.
  5. Shipra River: The Shipra, also known as the Kshipra, is a river in Madhya Pradesh state of central India

Must-Try foods :

  • Grilled Lobster: Indulge in succulent grilled lobster, perfectly seasoned and served with melted butter.
  • Fresh Tropical Fruits: Sample an abundance of fresh tropical fruits, including juicy mangoes, sweet pineapples, and refreshing papayas.
  • Coconut Curry: Delight in the flavors of a creamy coconut curry, infused with aromatic herbs and spices.
  • Exotic Seafood Platter: Treat yourself to a decadent seafood platter featuring a variety of locally sourced fish, shrimp, and scallops.
  • Pina Colada: Sip on a classic Pina Colada cocktail, made with rum, coconut cream, and pineapple juice, garnished with a fresh pineapple wedge.
